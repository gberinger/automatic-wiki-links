The British folk rock band *Trees* included a version of Glasgerion in The Garden of Jane Delawney, their 1970 debut album. Other renditions include The Flying Cloud's 1977 album, Traditional Music From Ireland, England, And Scotland (as Jack Orion), on Fairport Convention's 1978 album, Tipplers Tales (again with Dave Swarbrick on fiddle) and on Galley Beggar's 2015 album, Silence & Tears.. 'Jack Orion' also appears Fay Hield and The Hurricane Party's third album Old Adam (2016).
