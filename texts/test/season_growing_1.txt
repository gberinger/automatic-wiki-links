Compared with Teignmouth, which is on the coast about 22 miles (35 km) to the east, the average maximum and minimum temperatures are 3.0 °C (5.4 °F) and 2.6 °C (4.7 °F) lower respectively, and frost is at least five times as frequent.[12] On the highest ground, in the north of the moor, the growing *season* is less than 175 days – this contrasts with some 300 days along most of the south coast of the county.
