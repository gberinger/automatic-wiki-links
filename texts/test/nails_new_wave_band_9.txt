The bands with whom Certain General appeared in the early- and mid-eighties is a veritable list of many of the top independent rock bands of the era: Green on Red, Rain Parade, Mission of Burma, Gun Club, Oingo Boingo, New Order, Rank and File, The Rezillos, The Bongos, The *Nails* , Gang of Four, R.E.M., The Sisters of Mercy, Way of the West, Medium Medium, and Raybeats.
